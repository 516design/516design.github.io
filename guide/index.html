Applying your new decal

Clean

Clean the surface of your MacBook to ensure a smooth and secure application. Be sure the surface is completely dry before you attempt to apply your decal.

Remove

Remove the white backing from the decal. Avoid touching the sticky part of the decal while handling it.

Place

Place your decal on the surface of your MacBook, being sure to align it perfectly around the logo. Press firmly around the decal to set it in place.

Peel
