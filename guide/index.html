Applying your new decal

Clean

Clean the surface of your MacBook to ensure a smooth and secure application. Be sure the surface is completely dry before you attempt to apply your decal.

Remove

Remove the white backing. Try to avoid touching the sticky part of the decal while handling it.

Place

Place your decal on the surface of your MacBook, being sure to align it perfectly around the Apple logo. It may help to slightly open your computer and allow the light to guide you during the application. Press firmly around the decal to set it in place.

Peel

Note: Only follow this last step if you are applying a black, vinyl decal.
